Who am I?

My name is Dana and I am a web developer living in Montreal, Canada.

In November 2015 I started learning HTML, CSS and Python, then dived into JavaScript. And discovering the fun of styling visual elements and the satisfaction of solving programming challenges made everything change for me.

Since then I have spent countless hours and days learning and practicing - jQuery, ReactJS, SASS, Computer Science fundamentals - with one goal in mind: to become a professional web developer.

I bring with me general tech knowledge from my work as IT recruiter, self-discipline and information search skills from my university studies, and a curious, logical and mathematical mind. Combined with my dedication and pleasure for programming, these have helped me make fast progress and develop increasingly complex projects that you can find on this site.

I am open to front end development challenges where I can contribute with my enthusiasm and skills and keep learning and growing as a programmer.
